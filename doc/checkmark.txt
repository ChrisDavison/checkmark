INTRODUCTION                                    *checkmark*


A simple utility to toggle, uncheck, or remove checkboxes from markdown documents.


MAPS                                            *checkmark-maps*

                                                *checkmark-<leader>-x*
                                                *checkmark-v-<leader>-x*
{visual}<leader-x>                   
<leader>-x             Toggle checkbox on current line, or visual selection


                                                *checkmark-<leader>-X*
                                                *checkmark-v-<leader>-X*
{visual}<leader-X>                   
<leader>-X             Remove checkbox on current line, or visual selection


COMMANDS                                          *checkmark-commands*

:CheckToggle           Toggle between empty, and ticked checkbox (on line, 
                       range, or visual). If no checkbox exists, will add an 
                       empty checkbox.

:CheckRotate           Rotate between NO checkbox, empty, and ticked checkbox 
                       (on line, range, or visual)

:Uncheck               Remove checkboxes from the current line, range, or 
                       visual selection

:RMCHeck               Remove checkboxes from the entire document
